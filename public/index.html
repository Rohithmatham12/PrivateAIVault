PrivateAIVault

AI redaction + Midnight zero-knowledge commitments. Sensitive data is processed and provably committed, never exposed.

Zero-knowledge · Private by design

Step 1 · Raw sensitive record

This text is processed locally by the AI redaction step. It is never sent to the Midnight contract. Only its commitment is.

Step 2 · AI-redacted output

SHA-256 commitment

This hash, not the raw text above, is the only value about to be sent to the Midnight smart contract.

Step 3 · On-chain record (real Compact circuit, one of many)

This deployed contract holds an unbounded number of independent records, one per commitment. Committing another record above adds a new one; it never overwrites this one.

Step 4 · Verify a candidate record

Anyone holding what they believe is the true original can check it against the on-chain commitment, without exposing their candidate to anyone else.

Step 5 · Prove a redaction threshold, without revealing the count

The exact number of redacted spans is never written to the ledger. This proves a policy threshold ("at least N sensitive fields were redacted") using that count as a private witness, disclosing only the yes/no answer.
